Main Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

The captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy is a cheerful and determined boy whose dream is to find the One Piece and become the Pirate King. He has the powers of the Gum-Gum Fruit.

Roronoa Zoro

A master swordsman and Luffy's first mate, Zoro wields three swords at once. His ambition is to become the greatest swordsman in the world.

Nami

The crew's navigator, Nami has a talent for mapping and navigation. Her dream is to draw a complete map of the world.

Usopp

The sniper of the crew, Usopp is known for his cunning and resourcefulness. He dreams of becoming a brave warrior of the sea like his father.

Sanji

The crew's cook, Sanji is a master of culinary arts and martial arts. He dreams of finding the All Blue, a legendary sea where all fish species exist.
